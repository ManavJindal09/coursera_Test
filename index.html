Chole Bhature

Chole bhature is a food dish originating from northern India. It is a combination of chana masala and bhatura, a fried bread made from maida. There is a distinct Punjabi variant of the dish. Chhole bhature is often eaten as a breakfast dish, sometimes accompanied with lassi.
